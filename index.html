Optimize your gardening

Building a schedule around ensuring that all your plants are watered is actually pretty difficult. Florish helps to solve those problems by providing and easy to use interface for creating a plant watering schedule that will remind users to water their plants on a regular basis.

Features & Benefits

Pleasing Usability

Make an account with your email and phone number, then add your plants and other data, like their species name, plant type, and other specifics that make sense to keep track of. Have all of it visible and organized in one place.

Customized Notifications

Create a watering schedule for each plant and/or group of plants, and be notified by phone when it's time to give them a little TLC. We won't let you neglect your plants ever again.

Data-driven Care

Create and save snapshots of your plants' growth, view time-lapses, and also view metrics that kept track of your plants' health and wellness by looking at numbers of watering and other user-set variables.
