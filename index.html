Never Forget To Water Your Plants Again!


Finally, a water reminder app for your plants


It’s easy to forget to water your plants, but we can change that. Water My Plants reminds you to water plants through notifications and on a schedule you control. We also give you a plant gallery that you can update as your plant collection grows.
